Lordzeck and Gloria, two souls who had shared their childhood in this peaceful village, embarked on a journey that would length a decade of separation, only to find their paths crossing once again.

Their story began in the halls of Kigamba Primary School, where they would catch sights of each other daily, sharing friendly smiles and occasional greetings. As they grew older, they continued their education together at Kiyuni Secondary School, though they were not in the same class.

In their younger years, Lordzeck and Gloria attended the same Kigamba Primary School, walking the dusty paths to their classrooms and exchanging looks that sparked an interest within them.

Their paths converged further as they both continued their education at Kiyuni Secondary School. It was at Kiyuni that they had their last meeting in 2013, when they took a photo together, capturing a brief moment frozen in time. Little did they know that life would soon take them on different routes, causing them to lose touch with each other.

As the years passed, their memories of that crucial photo together began to fade, and life's responsibilities led them in separate directions. Even though they were in the same schools, they had never been in the same class, and as the years went by, the chances of seeing each other again grew slimmer.

It wasn't until early December 1st, 2022, that fate decided to intervene. Lordzeck mostly known as Zeki by most local friends found himself scrolling through Facebook, recalling about his past, when he stumbled upon a familiar name - Gloria, affectionately known as Liika. Memories of their school days rushed back, and he couldn't resist sending her a friend request, hoping she might remember him too.

Chapter 2: The Continue

The second chapter of Lordzeck and Gloria's love story unfolded on December 1st, 2023, marking a year since their virtual reunion after nearly a decade apart. This day held significant meaning as Gloria's contract abroad came to a close, and plans were underway for her return to Uganda. Lordzeck eagerly anticipated their reunion in the physical world, filled with joy and excitement at the prospect of creating new memories together.

However, what was supposed to be a day of celebration and their first anniversary since meeting virtually took an unexpected turn. Lordzeck had organized online festivities to commemorate the special milestone, but Gloria's message brought a surprising and unsettling revelation that shook the foundation of their bond.

In her message, Gloria made a heartfelt but heartbreaking decision. She expressed her belief that there was no future for them as a couple and proposed that they part ways romantically while maintaining a friendship. She suggested that Lordzeck reconcile with his previous partner, as her heart was leading her away from continuing their relationship.

As Lordzeck read these words on his phone, he was overcome by silence and a flood of emotions. The weight of Gloria's unexpected plea hit him hard, making him feel insignificant and lost in the world. He pleaded with her, hoping it was a joke and begging for a different answer. However, Gloria remained resolute, unable to change her decision.

This revelation shattered Lordzeck's heart, leaving him to reflect on the immense pain caused by this unexpected turn of events. In his broken state, he begged Gloria to reconsider and give it more thought, fearing the overwhelming agony consuming him. But despite his pleas, Gloria remained firm in her desire to transition their relationship into a friendship.

The suddenness and unpredictability of Gloria's decision left Lordzeck grappling with a whirlwind of emotions, struggling to accept the reality of what was happening. As Gloria stood by her choice, Lordzeck found himself faced with the daunting task of letting go of a deeply cherished love and preparing for a new chapter in their story—a chapter marked by the heartbreaking departure from their romantic bond.

Their relationship began to deteriorate from that day, just one day after December 1st. In their last video call, Lordzeck and Gloria had a frank conversation where Gloria realized that Lordzeck was deeply heartbroken. Lordzeck confessed to Gloria that she had been testing his love for her, despite repeatedly expressing that their relationship should end. However, Lordzeck insisted on continuing and expressed unwavering trust and commitment.

Unfortunately, within the same week, Gloria began making preparations to return to Uganda without prior notice. Lordzeck noticed Gloria's prolonged absence on WhatsApp, as she had been offline for an unusually long time. Lordzeck decided to call her directly and was surprised when the call connected, but Gloria did not answer. Lordzeck continued calling until Gloria finally picked up and confirmed that she was back in Uganda. She explained that she hadn't been able to inform Lordzeck earlier because her boss unexpectedly provided her with a ticket at 2 a.m., prompting her to leave immediately. Lordzeck listened attentively, trying to understand her explanation. They had plans to meet at the airport, but it didn't happen. Gloria assured Lordzeck that she would call soon and ended the conversation.

However, she didn't fulfill her promise to call later that evening, causing Lordzeck to feel anxious. Lordzeck decided to call her, and when she answered, Gloria casually informed him that she was still with her husband and would call him when she reached home. Lordzeck had to accept her words, but it felt like a sharp dagger piercing his heart.

On the same day, I waited for her call to let me know if she had arrived home, but she didn't contact me. The following day, I made multiple calls to her, but she didn't answer. In an attempt to reach her, I sent her a text message, but there was still no reply. I realized that she was no longer interested in talking to me, so I sent her a message asking her to extend my greetings to her family, particularly her sister, who used to be my close friend during school.

However, she didn't respond to any of my messages sent through WhatsApp, SMS, or calls. After two days of silence, I decided to write her a letter on WhatsApp, as I noticed that she was reading my messages without replying. In the letter, I humbly requested her to make one promise to me, giving me hope that we could restore our relationship to what it was before all these problems arose.

After reading the letter, she finally called me and expressed her displeasure at receiving such a long message. I explained that everything I wrote came from the depths of my heart, but she didn't listen and failed to provide a clear reason for not replying to my messages. She only mentioned that she had many visitors at home, leaving me bewildered by the constant stream of visitors and the lack of time for her to rest or reply to my messages. Despite this, I continued to hope for a resolution.

As time went on, I stopped sending her messages, and eventually, she messaged me, saying that she didn't have data to come online on WhatsApp. Without hesitation, I sent her 4k to subscribe to data bundles so we could chat, but she didn't make an effort to stay online. She also didn't inform me whether she received the credit on her phone or not.

From that day forward, I experienced a profound sense of isolation and decided to withdraw from all social interactions. Neglecting my responsibilities at work, I ignored calls from my concerned family and muted messages from business partners and friends. Overwhelmed by depression, hopelessness, and a feeling of worthlessness, I struggled to cope with the challenges life presented.

Then, one late December day after the Christmas season, she finally called me. Surprised by her sudden contact, I expressed my frustration at her previous disregard for my attempts to communicate. She explained that she was still at her sister's place and lacked the funds to travel. In response, I immediately sent her money and warm regards for her mother.

However, my assumption that she had already reached her mother's place proved incorrect. After that day, she once again ceased answering my calls and replying to my messages. When I pressed for an explanation, she claimed to be busy with visitors and promised to speak at a more convenient time.

I tried to take it in stride, but as time passed, I grew exhausted and felt compelled to put an end to the situation before unknowingly succumbing to it. Before making my final decision to terminate everything, I composed a lengthy letter on WhatsApp, proposing a meeting to discuss our issues and make mutually beneficial decisions.

In response, she dismissively called me, asking me to stop sending lengthy messages. She agreed to meet but expressed her lack of funds to travel. I asked her to wait for two days, and on Wednesday, I sent her the necessary transportation money. She confirmed receipt of the funds, and the plan was for her to leave her mother's place on Thursday, spend the night at her sister's, and arrive at my location early Friday morning.

Excitement overwhelmed me as I eagerly awaited the long-awaited reunion with my dear friend after 11 years. I shared my anticipation with close friends, who supported and assisted me in preparing for the occasion. I bought fresh food, branded t-shirts for twins, displayed our cherished photo from 2013, and prepared my home to welcome this special person. I even purchased cosmetics, chocolates, perfumes, a dress, and massage oil as gifts. My heart soared, as though I were about to meet the queen of my heart.

However, to my dismay, I received no updates from her regarding her journey. Despite my assumption that she was planning a surprise, I grew anxious as the afternoon hours passed. Finally, I decided to call her and inquire about her progress. She explained that she had left her mother's place with the intention of spending the night at her sister's but had ended up at a friend's place in the village, where she was surrounded by many people and exhausted from the late hour at which she had fallen asleep.

I urged her to prepare and come the following day, Saturday. Reluctantly, she agreed to make an effort. I waited for her arrival, but she failed to come and instead provided a series of excuses. She postponed our meeting to the following Sunday, then Monday, and eventually to the upcoming Friday.

My heart shattered, and I sent her a heartfelt voice message on WhatsApp, expressing my deep disappointment and informing her that I was no longer interested in pursuing a relationship. I also confronted her about her refusal to answer my calls and her audacity to reject them or keep them busy. She responded with indifference, accepting my decision to end the relationship without attempting to understand my feelings. She even remarked that I hadn't given her an opportunity to explain what had transpired.

When I inquired about the reasons behind her behavior, she casually mentioned that her husband had discovered she was in his home village without informing him, prompting him to come and investigate. In her explanation, she callously stated that she had been with him during the nights when I called, as she didn't want him to obtain my number and bother her. I questioned how he had managed to find her if it was a surprise encounter, considering she had been traveling since the previous Thursday and it was now another Thursday.

I poured out my pain at the realization that everything she had explained was simply hurtful. She responded by stating that if it hurt me so much, then it would have hurt her husband even more if I had taken her away from him. Shocked, I asked if she had merely used me and no longer needed me. She callously replied that I had been her backup plan and that she had no issues with her husband.

Her words struck me like a dagger to the heart. I felt as though my world was collapsing around me, and the woman I once believed genuinely loved me had become heartless, insensitive, and indifferent to my well-being. Her suggestion that I forgive her, citing the fact that I had forgiven a previous love who had hurt me, left me speechless.

The pain of wasted time, resources, and emotional investment overwhelmed me. It was clear that my trust had been shattered, and the friendship we once shared could never be restored. The realization hit me hard, but it also opened my eyes to the importance of valuing myself and surrounding myself with people who genuinely care about my well-being.

In the aftermath of this painful experience, I made a commitment to focus on healing and rebuilding my life. I sought solace in the support of my true friends and family, who stood by me during this difficult time. With their encouragement, I gradually regained my sense of self-worth and rediscovered my passions and goals.

Later, after about three months, her best friend, whom she had introduced to me earlier, approached me and pleaded for forgiveness. She explained that Gloria herself had sent voice notes through her, expressing her desire for us to reconcile and be together again. Despite feeling a tug of forgiveness and a desire to mend things, I chose to remain silent and did not respond to her proposition.

I saw forgiving her as a betrayal to myself, as I wanted to prioritize my own healing process and remain loyal and true to myself. Our communication on WhatsApp dwindled to simple greetings like "hey" and "hi," a far cry from the days when we used to chat day and night. Although her best friend continued to insist and request forgiveness and understanding, and Gloria herself made the same plea, I remained undecided.

I recognized that forgiveness is a complex and personal journey, and I needed time to process my emotions and determine what was truly best for my well-being. For now, I chose to maintain distance and focus on my own healing and growth.

While the scars from this betrayal may linger, I am determined to move forward and embrace the lessons I have learned. I have come to understand that I deserve respect, honesty, and genuine connections in my relationships. I will no longer settle for anything less.

As I reflect on this chapter of my life, I am grateful for the strength and resilience that carried me through the darkest moments. I am reminded that life's challenges can be transformative, and even in the face of heartbreak, there is always the potential for growth and renewal.

Moving forward, I will cherish the lessons learned and use them as stepping stones towards a brighter and more fulfilling future. I will surround myself with those who uplift and support me, and I will continue to prioritize my own well-being. Though the pain may still linger, I am confident that I will emerge from this experience stronger and more resilient than ever before.
